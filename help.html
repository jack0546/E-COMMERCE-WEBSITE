Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order in the "Orders" section of your account. Once the supplier ships the goods, a tracking number will be provided.

What is Trade Assurance?

Trade Assurance is a free service that protects your orders from payment to delivery. It ensures quality and on-time shipment.

How do I contact a supplier?

Simply click the "Contact Supplier" button on any product detail page to start a conversation through our messaging system.
