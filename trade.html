Protect Your Orders from Payment to Delivery

Trade Assurance is a free service that creates trust between business buyers and suppliers. We provide a set of protections to ensure suppliers honor important details of your contract.

Payment Security

Your payment is held in escrow and released only after you confirm delivery.

Shipping Protection

Ensure your products are shipped on time, or get your money back.

Product Quality

Products must meet the quality standards specified in your contract.
